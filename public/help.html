Home

Sign In

In order to sign in to this application, go to the home page and click login. Proceed to log in with your TAMU account credentials.

Events Sign Up

To sign up for events, click on the events tab from home page and click on 'Confirm Attendance' to prove that you were at the event.

To remove yourself form an event, select 'Remove My Attendance'

Profile

You can edit your user information by clicking on the 'Edit Profile' button from the Home Page.

Points

Once you have signed up for an event, you will gain your points once the officers approve the attendance for that event.
